About Me

I'm a sixteen year old amateur photographer and computer programmer. I live on a ranch in North Texas and I play on the drumline in my school's marching band.

My Career in Photography

Photography was another thing that I'd been intereseted in for quite a bit of time, and after moving to the country to live on a ranch during Christmas of 2014, I've gained many opportunities to capture beautiful moments. I got my first real camera when I turned 16: an older (and discontinued) Nikon model. Since then, I've participated in online courses and experimented with ways to capture the perfect shot. I have a small gallery of about fifty photos of mine here.

My Career in Computer Programming

I started working with computers around the age of nine years old. I've had an interest in technology as long as I can remember, and, with being able to utilize the core concepts of technological design through computer programming, I have discovered my passion. I have experience in many different programming languages ranging from desktop programming to backend database management, and I plan to major in computer science in college.

Programming Languages and Applications

I have experience with C#, CSS3, HTML5, Java, Javascript, jQuery, PHP, Python, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and SQL. My favorite programming language is Python because of its modular nature and portability.

In addition to programming, I have experience with Adobe Photoshop, Apache Webserver, Eclipse IDE, Git, phpMyAdmin, mySQL, and the Linux CLI.
